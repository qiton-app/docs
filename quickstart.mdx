## Why we started Qiton ?

<Frame>
    <img
        src="./images/lotus_map.jpg"
        alt="Lotus map"
    />
</Frame>

> "Start winning with data that works."

Esports teams are drowning in massive, complex data files that are tough to process and harder to use.
With tight budgets and limited access to developers, organizations can't unlock the full potential of their data.

That's where we come in.
Our mission is simple: make esports data accessible, actionable and beautiful.
For coaches, managers and players alike.

With our fast, intuitive and powerful web platform, we transform overwhelming raw data into clear insights that fuel
smarter decisions, sharper strategies and stronger performances.

The future of esports performance starts here.
