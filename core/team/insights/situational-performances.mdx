These charts showcase your win rate in different scenarios within team matches, including 1v1, 1v2, and so on.

## Prerequisites

- An active account ([learn more](/get-started/setup))
- You have to select a team beforehand ([learn more](/core/team/root))

## Steps

Navigate to the **Insights** tab.

## Available statistics

- `Total wins & losses in advantage situations for the selected team`
- `Total wins & losses in disadvantage situations for the selected team`
- `Total wins & losses in equal situations for the selected team`
