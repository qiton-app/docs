## Available statistics

- `Wins percentage: Defuse`
- `Wins percentage: Bomb`

- `Wins percentage: Kills (pre-plant)`

## Available filters

Modify the scope of the provided statistics based on the following situations.
- `Attack & defense`
- `Attack`
- `Defense`
