Conversions display your win rate in various situations within the currently selected match, such as 1v1, 1v2, and so on.

Multiple conversions are available for each match:
- One globally
- One per sub-match

## Prerequisites

- An active account ([learn more](/get-started/setup))
- You have to select a match beforehand ([learn more](/core/match/root))

## Available statistics

- `Total wins & losses in 1v1 situations per team`
- `Total wins & losses in 1v2 situations per team`
- `Total wins & losses in 1v3 situations per team`
- `Total wins & losses in 1v4 situations per team`
- `Total wins & losses in 1v5 situations per team`

## Available filters

- `Advantage`
- `Disadvantage`
- `Equal`
