Let’s see which team manages its economy well and which doesn’t.

## Prerequisites

- An active account ([learn more](/get-started/setup))
- You have to select a team beforehand ([learn more](/core/team/root))

## Steps

Navigate to the **Economy** tab.

## Available statistics

- `Winrate in this head-to-head scenario (here force buys vs. other scenarios)`
- `Economic damage (% of loadout value lost by enemy thanks to your performance)`
- `Economic loss (% of loadout value you lost during this round)`
- `Ultimates wasted for both your team and enemy team`

## Available filters

- `Scenarios` (pistols, ecos, one man army, half buy, force buy or full buy)
- `Dates`
- `Tournaments`
- `Scrims`
- `Officials`
- `Maps`
