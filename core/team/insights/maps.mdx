Determine which maps your team performs better on.

## Prerequisites

- An active account ([learn more](/get-started/setup))
- You have to select a team beforehand ([learn more](/core/team/root))

## Steps

Navigate to the **Insights** tab.

- `Dates`
- `Tournaments`
- `Scrims`
- `Officials`
