    <Tab title="ACS">
        ACS stands for "Average combat score".
        The ACS is calculated based on various factors, including kills, deaths, assists, and the impact of abilities
        used during the game. A higher ACS generally indicates better performance.

    <Tab title="KDA">
        KDA stands for "Kills, deaths, assists".
        The KDA ratio is calculated by adding kills and assists, then dividing by the number of deaths. A high KDA
        generally indicates good performance, as it means the player has significantly contributed to the team while
        minimizing their own deaths.
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="ADR">
        ADR stands for "Average damage per round".
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="HS">
        HS stands for "Headshot".
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="KAST">
        KAST stands for "Kill, assist, survived, traded".
        The percentage of a player achieving at least one kill, assist, survival, or trading each round.
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="FK">
        FK stands for "First kill".
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="FD">
        FD stands for "First death".
